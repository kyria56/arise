A Message From Master Andre

Welcome to our martial arts family! We are thrilled to have you join Arise Champion Taekwondo and embark on this exciting journey in Tae Kwon Do. Our school is dedicated to providing a supportive and challenging environment where you can grow physically, mentally, and spiritually.

We are committed to helping you achieve your goals and reach your full potential. We’re excited to help you get started.

Etiquette & Rules

  • Respect: Show respect for instructors, fellow students, and the dojo.
  • Cleanliness: Keep the dojo clean and tidy.
  • Punctuality: Arrive on time for class. If you are late, wait for permission to join.
  • Hygiene: Keep yourself clean and your uniform well-maintained.
  • No Food or Drink: No food or drinks are allowed in the training area.
  • Bowing: Bow upon entering and leaving the training area.
  • Proper Attire: Always wear the appropriate uniform for class.

Your First 30 Days: Checklist

Use this checklist to make the most of your first 30 days at Arise.

  • Attend at least 2 classes per week.
  • Learn the basic stances and movements.
  • Get to know your instructors and classmates.
  • Review class material at home.
  • Familiarize yourself with the dojo etiquette and rules.
  • Ask questions and seek clarification when needed.
  • Set a personal goal for your training.
  • Explore our tools and resources to support your training.

The Arise TKD Membership Oath

  1. We, as members, are training our spirit and bodies according to the strict code.
  2. We, as members, are united in mutual friendship.
  3. We, as members, will comply with regulations and obey our instructor.
